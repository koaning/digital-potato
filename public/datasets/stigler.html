Stigler.

Calculate an optimal diet.

Purpose
This is a dataset that contains nutritional values of household commidities. The dataset was used in the "stigler"-problem where ones tries to figure out how to minimise the costs of food while still getting enough nutrients.
Links

This dataset is used in a number of places on our website.


    CVXpy
Source
This dataset was originally found in OR tools.
Contents

Name Description
commodity The name of the product.
unit The measurement unit used for quantify the product.
price_cents The 1939 price in cents.
calories Calories per unit of the commodity
protein_g Grams of protein per the unit of the commodity.
calcium_g Grams of calcium per the unit of the commodity.
iron_mg Miligrams of iron per the unit of the commodity.
vitamin_a_iu Vitamin A per the unit of the commodity.
vitamin_b1_mg Vitamin B1 per the unit of the commodity.
vitamin_b2_mg Vitamin B2 per the unit of the commodity.
niacin_mg Niacin per the unit of the commodity.
vitamin_c_mg Vitamin C per the unit of the commodity.
Download

You can download the dataset from the commandline.

wget https://calmcode.io/datasets/stigler.csv
